Works Artist Statement

I'm Anthony Ly, an Interdisciplinary Computing & Arts Major (ICAM) at University of California, San Diego, my goal actually isn't to become a traditional "artist". I'm pursuing my career in UI/UX software development. About my artworks, my journey into digital art began with my love of anime and games. Drawing fanart felt like a natural way to explore my creativity. Over time I experimented with two main approaches - vibrant, fully colored pieces and high contrast black & white sketches inspired by one of my favorite aritsts. While Ive since shifted most of my energy toward tech and UI/UX design, these early works taught me how to blend aesthetics and funciton, some insight that continues to inform my design philosophy today. This isn't really a main portfolio for me but rather my first project on UI/UX design. I coded this whole entire website myself and will try to continue to make more projects like this.
